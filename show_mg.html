Morning Glory

Listen in as we wake up campus...

Joining our flagship morning show is the best way to start out on URB.
Tune in at 9am daily to hear our presenters review the food in limetree, discuss the traffic on the way up to campus and generally talk about anything Bath. Easy listening and generally some bangers - your best way to wake up for morning lectures.
