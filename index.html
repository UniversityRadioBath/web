Flagship and Specialist Shows

Morning Glory and Afternoon delight are our flagship shows - running every weekday 9am-11pm and 1pm- 3pm. It's the easiest way to get involved without any experience. Come and give it a go! 

URBar tour (and other Events...) 

URB are famous for our socials and events. We run regular Bar Tours, nightly pres on campus during freshers' week and countless events throughout the year. Join one of our fornightly socials and get involved!     

DJs & URB Roadshow

URB is a great way to start out in the world of DJing. We play each tuesday at Cocktails in the SU and regularly feature at bars and clubs in the city.

Running an event and looking for music, speakers, and your own DJ? URB offer competitive prices, so get in touch.

Competitions

URB partner with some of the biggest names in Bath, to give you the change to win incredible prizes. We've given away TAKKA TAKKA, GBK, Turtle Bay and Brewhouse vouchers (just to name a few), VIP packages at Revs, Moles and Po Na Na, Komedia tickets... and that was all in a single week. Look out for Freshers'21 as we're sure to come back with a bang.

We're much more than just a radio station... 

Record a Podcast 

Grab some friends or foes and begin your podcast journey with URB. Open to all members of the University Community (ask about membership).

Roadshow - Hire Equipment, a DJ, or both.

Hire our speakers or decks for a set price, and save some money for your party or student society - on or off campus.

Present your own show

Fancy subjecting others to your own poor taste in music? Well, URB can help you do just that ;). 

We'll teach you how to use the desk and get you up to speed.    

Broadcast Engineering - Join our Tech Team 

Come take a look at our broadcast setup - we're running a Livewire network with Audio over AES67, a bunch of servers and some pretty cool tricks. Take a look round :).
